AMBER LI

Raised in sunny San Diego, Amber lived the first 18 years in relatively the same place. She's only moved one but it was a long time ago so she doesn't remember

Thinking she was supposed to follow in her parents footsteps and pursue a career in STEM so she studied hard till the last year of high school when the...

fire nation attacked.

Only the Avatar, master of all four elements, could stop them, but when the world needed him most, he vanished.

A couple months passed, and Amber discovered the new Avatar, an airbender named Aang.

And he taught her that she should go to art school and follow her gut. So she did and here we are.
